1. Data Protection

DeepShield AI operates on a strict zero-retention capability policy for unauthenticated requests. Media files submitted for evaluation (images/videos) are buffered solely within secure volatile execution memory bounds during model inference. Transformed numerical tensor values are flushed immediately upon delivery of predictions.

2. Privacy Controls

Facial bounding structures cropped during MTCNN mapping layers isolate regions of interest (ROI) exclusively for structural anomaly calculation. Biological coordinates are processed strictly inside sandboxed environments and are never correlated with identity verification data streams.

DeepShield aligns with the European Union Artificial Intelligence Act specifications and GDPR compliance guidelines concerning biometric data handling, guaranteeing users transparency over model training weights and analytical auditability pathways.
