1. Terms of Use

DeepShield AI provides media verification interfaces for forensic analysis. Users agree to process only assets for which they retain appropriate legal evaluation authority. Computational reverse engineering, weights extraction, or distillation attempts targeting DeepShield neural network nodes are strictly prohibited.

2. Model Performance & Accuracy

DeepShield AI models (Fusion RGB+FFT V2 and Xception+BiLSTM) generate statistical confidence weights indicating synthetic likelihood. While optimized for accuracy (94.4%), outputs represent localized evaluations of structural anomalies and should not be used as absolute legal proof of authenticity.

3. Rate Limits & Abuse Prevention

Automated scraping of the dashboard or parallelized multi-stream ingestion without active API licenses is monitored and restricted. The platform reserves the right to isolate and terminate access to operational operational nodes for entities exhibiting high-frequency scraping behaviors.
